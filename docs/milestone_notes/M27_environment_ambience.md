## B. Problem statement (verified at audit)

1. **Flat service screens.** All six town-service states draw a single
   `ClearBackground(solid)` (`InnState` `{20,16,24}`; `ItemShopState`,
   `EquipShopState`, `TrainingHallState`, `ScoreboardState` `{16,16,24}`;
   `GuildState` `{16,16,26}`), so every service looks like the same empty box.
2. **Same-y ambience.** Per-theme ambience *is* wired (`DungeonState`
   selects keep/mine/forest beds; `TownState` selects the town bed) — the four
   `WriteAmbience` beds in `generate_audio.ps1` simply sound too alike.
3. **Ambience on the wrong slider.** Ambience volume multiplies by
   `musicVolume_` at two sites (`AudioManager::startAmbience` and
   `::setVolumes`), so the **Music** slider controls it and no SFX-side control
   exists.

## C. Owner decisions (recorded at planning, reaffirmed here)

- Ambience volume is routed to the existing **SFX slider**. **No new Settings
  field, no save migration** (rejected a dedicated ambience slider).

## D. Goals

- Each service screen reads as a distinct place while keeping **all overlaid
  text within contrast/overflow budgets** (the binding constraint).
- The three theme ambience beds are distinguishable in a blind listen.
- The **SFX** slider demonstrably moves ambience; the **Music** slider no longer
  does.

## E. Non-goals

- Town tilemap/layout changes; new music tracks; a dedicated ambience slider
  (explicitly rejected); any gameplay/AI/generation/scoring/save change.

## F. Design

### F.1 Service backgrounds (legibility-first)

- Six subtle full-screen (426×240) backgrounds generated by
  `generate_textures.ps1`, one per service, keyed to the service's mood: a dark
  gradient on the state's existing base tone + a low-contrast themed motif kept
  toward the edges/top, away from central text. Base stays near the current
  fill so M12 text contrast is preserved (art bible §7).
- Drawn via a new `ui::drawSceneBackground(resources, id, fallbackColor, w, h)`
  helper: fills the fallback colour, then draws the texture scaled to the
  virtual screen if the catalog has it — **missing texture ⇒ the old solid
  fill**, so nothing regresses. Manifest ids `bg.inn`, `bg.item_shop`,
  `bg.equip_shop`, `bg.training_hall`, `bg.scoreboard`, `bg.guild`.
- Each of the six states swaps its `ClearBackground(color)` for the helper with
  the same colour as fallback.

### F.2 Ambience beds

- Rework the four `WriteAmbience` beds so they separate clearly by spectral
  character and rhythm (distinct lowpass cutoffs, LFO rates, and drone/chirp
  content), keeping them quiet and seam-consistent. Deterministic
  (byte-identical reruns). Final acceptance is the owner's listen.

### F.3 Ambience → SFX slider

- Change the two ambience gain sites in `AudioManager.cpp` from `musicVolume_`
  to `sfxVolume_`. `setVolumes` already re-applies on any slider change, so the
  SFX slider updates ambience live. No API/schema change.

## K. As-implemented record (2026-07-20)

Implemented on top of the M26 approval commit.

- **Backgrounds:** `generate_textures.ps1` extended (appended, RNG-reseeded so
  existing/enemy PNGs stay byte-identical) with six 426×240 service backgrounds
  under `textures/backgrounds/`. New `ui::drawSceneBackground` helper +
  `bg.{inn,item_shop,equip_shop,training_hall,scoreboard,guild}` manifest rows;
  the six service states swapped `ClearBackground(color)` for the helper (same
  colour as fallback). Verified via capture that all six render distinct and
  every line of text stays legible.
- **Ambience beds (redesigned for real distinctness).** A first retune that
  only varied the shared filtered-noise recipe was **not audibly different**
  (owner feedback; confirmed by measurement — all four fingerprinted nearly
  identically). The beds were rebuilt as **layered synthesis** — a quiet/loud
  noise bed plus a per-place drone and *recurring, above-the-bed events* — so
  each place has its own signature: **town** = quiet bright breeze + frequent
  bird whistles; **keep** = a loud, hollow, slowly-beating low drone
  (98/99/49 Hz) with distant moans; **mine** = a metallic hum (147/294/440 Hz)
  under eight regular echoing drips; **forest** = a busy, bright, fluttering
  leaf rustle with low owl hoots. Objective check (post-fix): transient-event
  counts town 35 / keep 0 / mine 8 / forest 28, and low-band fraction
  2% / 91% / 57% / 41% — four clearly separated signatures.
  - **Bug found and fixed:** the cross-function array build hit a PowerShell
    gotcha — `return $out` unrolled the bed to `Object[]`, so every
    `[double[]]` event helper mutated a throwaway copy and the drones/birds/
    drips never mixed in (the bed was pure noise). Fixed with the `return ,$out`
    idiom (keeps `Double[]`, mutations persist).
  - **Loudness balance:** keep's sustained drone measured ~11 dB hotter (RMS)
    than the sparse beds, so `ambience.keep` manifest volume was trimmed
    0.35 → 0.24; owner can fine-tune by ear.
  - Music and SFX WAVs verified **byte-identical**; ambience deterministic on
    rerun.
- **Slider reroute:** the two ambience gain sites in `AudioManager.cpp`
  (`startAmbience`, `setVolumes`) now multiply by `sfxVolume_`. No new Settings
  field, no save migration.
- **Validation (this session):** full suite **252/252**; `--capture` **23/23
  overflow-clean** with backgrounds resolving (no placeholder/default-font
  warnings); Debug + Release both build clean; both generators rerun
  byte-identical (with the intended ambience-bed and background changes).

### Deviations from the plan

- **Extended `generate_textures.ps1`** (not a sibling) for backgrounds — same
  append+reseed discipline as M26, so no previously-committed PNG changed.
- **Backgrounds are deliberately subtle** (dark tinted gradient + low-alpha
  edge-biased motif) rather than detailed scenes: legibility of overlaid text
  was the binding constraint and the six services vary text placement. Motif
  recognisability and overall look are the owner's art call.
- The ambience reroute is device-side raylib and not unit-tested (consistent
  with the rest of `AudioManager`); it is owner-validated by ear + slider check.
