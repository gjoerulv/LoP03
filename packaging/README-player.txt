CRYSTAL DUNGEONS
a 16-bit-inspired dungeon-score roguelite


RUNNING THE GAME

Run CrystalDungeons.exe. No installation is needed; keep the data and
assets folders next to the exe. To uninstall, delete this folder.

Saves and settings live in your user folder (not here), so replacing or
moving this folder never touches your progress.


THE GAME IN ONE PARAGRAPH

Build a party of four, prepare in town, and enter seeded dungeons. Enemy
teams are always visible - read their danger label before you commit.
Fight through the gates, claim guarded chests, weigh the room events, and
defeat the boss. Your score rewards finishing the dungeon in the FEWEST
BATTLE TURNS - decisive play, not grinding. Then spend your spoils and go
deeper.


CONTROLS (defaults - remappable in Settings)

  Move / Navigate     Arrow keys or WASD     D-Pad / Left stick
  Confirm             Enter or Space         A
  Cancel / Back       Esc or Backspace       B
  Menu / Pause        Tab                    Start
  Details / Info      C                      Y

The in-game Controls screen always shows your current bindings. Settings
offers volume, window mode, battle speed, message speed, effect intensity,
High Contrast, and tutorial-prompt controls.


KNOWN LIMITATIONS

- English only.
