# Asset Credits and Provenance

Every asset shipped under `assets/` must have an entry here **before** it is
referenced by `manifest.json`: file, role id(s), source/author, license, and
any required attribution text. A shipped external asset without an entry is a
release blocker (M24 checks). Original assets are marked as such.
